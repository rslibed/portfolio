About Environmindful

Climate change web application that displays Air Quality Info, Energy Production Levels, recent news articles, and local weather in major US cities.

Built with HTML5, CSS3, and jQuery.

Utilized multiple AJAX network requests to Google Maps API, News API, Air Quality API, Carma API, and the Weather API. Implemented Google Fusion Tables to display visual representation of carbon emissions.

Implemented Bootstrap for styling & mobile responsiveness
